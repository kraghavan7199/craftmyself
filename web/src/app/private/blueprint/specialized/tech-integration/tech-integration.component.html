Technology Integration: Your Digital Toolkit

Leverage modern technology to track data, analyze performance, and streamline your fitness journey. Use these tools to work smarter, not just harder.

Tracking Apps

Data is power. These apps help you quantify your efforts.

  • Nutrition: MyFitnessPal, Cronometer, MacroFactor (for its smart coach).
  • Training: Strong, Hevy, Jefit. Log every set, rep, and weight to ensure progression.

Wearable Devices

Monitor your recovery, which is half the battle.

  • Sleep & Recovery: WHOOP, Oura Ring. Track sleep stages, HRV, and resting heart rate to gauge your readiness to train.
  • Activity: Apple Watch, Garmin. Useful for tracking daily steps and cardio sessions.

Video & Form Analysis

Your phone is your best coach. Film your main lifts from the side and back.

  • Self-Analysis: Check for bar path, depth, and technique breakdown.
  • Tools: Apps like Iron Path can track the bar's path for you.
  • Get Feedback: Post form checks to knowledgeable online communities for constructive criticism.
