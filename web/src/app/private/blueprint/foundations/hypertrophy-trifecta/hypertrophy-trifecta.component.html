The Science of Growth: The Trifecta of Hypertrophy

These are the primary stressors that, when paired with strategic rest, drive growth. Mechanical tension is the undisputed king.

Mechanical Tension

The #1 driver. Force from heavy lifting (progressive overload) is the most potent stimulus for muscle growth.

Muscle Damage

Micro-damage from training initiates a repair and adaptation response, building muscle back stronger.

Metabolic Stress

The "pump" from pushing muscles to their metabolic limits, which contributes to growth but less than tension.
