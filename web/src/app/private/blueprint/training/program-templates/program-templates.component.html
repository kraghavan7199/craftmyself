Sample Program Templates

These templates put the principles into practice. Choose the one that best fits your training age and schedule. Progression is key: for Novices, add a small amount of weight each session. For Intermediates/Advanced, use the progression models outlined earlier.

Novice: Full Body (3x per Week)

Alternating Workout A and B on non-consecutive days (e.g., Mon/Wed/Fri).

Workout A

  • Squat: 3 sets of 5 reps
  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 5 reps
  • Barbell Row: 3 sets of 5 reps

Workout B

  • Squat: 3 sets of 5 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 5 reps
  • Deadlift: 1 set of 5 reps

Intermediate: Upper/Lower Split (4x per Week)

Example Schedule: Upper (Mon), Lower (Tues), Rest (Wed), Upper (Thurs), Lower (Fri).

Upper Body

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Barbell Row: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-ups/Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 2 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 2 sets of 12-15 reps

Lower Body

  • Squat: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 10-15 reps

Advanced: Push/Pull/Legs (6x per Week)

Example Schedule: Push (Mon), Pull (Tues), Legs (Wed), Push (Thurs), Pull (Fri), Legs (Sat). Focus on strength one day and hypertrophy the next (e.g. Heavy Bench on Push 1, Incline DB Press on Push 2).

Push Day

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 4-6 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Incline DB Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Pull Day

  • Weighted Pull-ups: 4 sets of 4-6 reps
  • Barbell Row: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • T-Bar Row: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Face Pulls: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Barbell Curls: 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Leg Day

  • Squat: 4 sets of 4-6 reps
  • Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 8-12 reps/leg
  • Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Seated Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 10-15 reps
