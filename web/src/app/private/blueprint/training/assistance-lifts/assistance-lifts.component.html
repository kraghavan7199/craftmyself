Strategic Assistance Lifts

Assistance exercises are not for "confusing the muscles" or just adding variety. Their purpose is to strengthen the muscles that support your main lifts, address weak points, and ensure balanced development.

Chin-ups / Pull-ups

The king of upper body pulling exercises. They build the lats, upper back, and biceps, creating the stability needed for a strong bench press and balancing out pushing movements.

Dips

A fantastic pressing movement that heavily targets the triceps, chest, and anterior deltoids. An excellent complement to the Bench Press and Press for building pushing strength.

Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs)

A pure hip-hinge movement that directly targets the hamstrings and glutes. Crucial for building the posterior chain strength needed for a powerful squat and deadlift.
